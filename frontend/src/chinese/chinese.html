Chinese Language Resources

Chinese Grammar Wiki

A resource which compiles important Mandarin Chinese grammar points, starting from beginner going up to upper intermediate.

Skritter

An application for learning how to write Chinese characters. This includes handwriting practice and corrections, a spaced-repetition flashcard system, as well as a way to track personal progress.

The Chairman's Bao

A news-based graded reader where users can select Chinese news stories based on level, and learn keywords and grammar points used in the stories.

Purple Culture Chinese Tools

A compilation of multiple free resources to learn Mandarin Chinese.
